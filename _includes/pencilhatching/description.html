Pencil Hatching is an Android app with which you can interactively transform your photos into hand drawings. The realistic pencil strokes are automatically oriented according to the image content. Artistic contours facilitate a sharp drawing. In contrast to other apps, the virtual brush of Pencil Hatching let you become an artist by locally adjusting the drawing.
